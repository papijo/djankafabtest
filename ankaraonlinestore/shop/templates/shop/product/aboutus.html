About Us

Our Story

Ankara Fabrics and More is one of the fastest growing fabric dealerships in Ghana. With Our vibrant, youthful, elegant and competitively priced fabrics, we are a class above other distributors. Our range of products include African Print, Kente, Senator Fabrics, Voile, Silk Satin, Lace and Asoebi Fabrics. We are present in 3 West African cities namely Accra, Lome and Lagos.

Our Social Responsibility

Ankara Fabrics isn't all about profit making or getting the next client; we are also a company deeply committed to improving our host communities and it's citizens.
It is in light of this way that we partnered with big time multi-media to feed 5000 street children on a project dubbed the BOAFO HAPPY CHILD PROJECT.
